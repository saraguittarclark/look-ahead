UX and the Elementary Art Classroom: User Experience Research and Strategy

Goal:
Create and implement a unique elementary studio art curriculum with a Research and Development/growth mindset consistent with best practices in education, based on National Standards of Art.

Summary of Impact: 75% growth in student engagement during art class, student summative scores improved by 80%.

UX Methods used: Understand, Define, Sketch, Decide, Prototype, Validate - User journeys, experience mapping, qualitative data analysis, SWOT, golden path, cognitive walkthrough, HEART framework, usability testing.

I reviewed our Mission and Vision statements in the lens of our current Art Curriculum. "Was there a problem that needs solving?"
I generated user personas, conducted user interviews, and created then revised experience maps to discover the emotional and physical barriers that could be addressed in a curriculum change.
